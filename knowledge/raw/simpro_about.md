## Our story

In 2002, an electrical business owner teamed up with a software engineering student. Why? Because the software available didn’t work for the trades. It was built for people who had never set foot on a job site.

So we built our own. Software that actually works in the field – and the office. Tools that solve real problems. Systems that don’t waste your time or cut into your margins.

Fast forward: Today Simpro is the leading global AI-first operating system for residential and commercial trades.

![composition showing Simpro running on a mobile device and a tablet device, with Global Leader - Field Service Software badge](https://www.simprogroup.com/user/pages/company/about-us/03._industry-leader/simpro-comp-with-global-leader-badge-2x-min.png)

## Why We Exist

Because trades are the backbone of the economy. You keep the lights on. You keep water flowing. You keep buildings safe. You deserve profits that match the value of your work. Our mission is simple: **double contractor profitability**. Not “optimize,” not “revolutionize,” not any of that buzzword mumbo jumbo. Double it. Period.
