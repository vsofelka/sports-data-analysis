### What is Simpro:

Simpro is field service management software designed for those in the trade service industry, such as electricians, HVAC installers, and plumbers. It has tools for tasks such as writing quotes, scheduling jobs, managing inventory, and financial reporting.

### Why we like Simpro:

Simpro shines for its billing and invoicing features, as well as its calendar management capabilities. It also provides users with mobile access, which is key for service providers who spend many hours on the road and at job sites.

### Who should use Simpro:

Simpro is a good choice for organizations that have to manage teams of service professionals. It’s a solid fit for smaller companies, with 56% of reviewers hailing from organizations with between 11 and 200 employees.

## Simpro Key Sentiments

We analyzed 145 verified reviews for Simpro to find out what actual users really think.

Most Positive Sentiments

Field technician management

65% Positive

12% Neutral

24% Negative

Users find Simpro's mobile app useful for managing field technicians, offering real-time progress monitoring and easy task assignment. They appreciate the app's usability for adding time and materials on the go, which enhances efficiency. However, some users experience issues with service ticket creation and reporting limitations, particularly in areas with poor cell service.

Training support

63% Positive

19% Neutral

19% Negative

Reviewers commend Simpro for its extensive training process, which helps them navigate the software's robust features. They find the training sessions well-designed and appreciate the ongoing support that eases the transition to using Simpro. Users also highlight the knowledgeable trainers who ensure their team is comfortable and proficient with the platform.

Customization flexibility

57% Positive

36% Neutral

7% Negative

Reviewers acknowledge Simpro's extensive customization options, which allow them to tailor the software to their business needs. However, they also note that the customization process can be overwhelming and that certain areas, such as quote forms, require more flexibility. Users express a desire for more customizable permissions and template options to better suit their specific requirements.

Least Positive Sentiments

Costs

17% Positive

8% Neutral

75% Negative

Reviewers express concerns about the high costs associated with Simpro, citing expensive setup fees and a money-focused approach from the company. They feel that the software did not meet their expectations and found the overall experience costly and frustrating. Users advise potential customers to consider other options due to the significant financial investment required.

Job costing

46% Positive

42% Neutral

12% Negative

Users appreciate Simpro's job costing capabilities, which provide visibility into project costs and profitability throughout the project lifecycle. They find the software effective in tracking labor, time, and expenses, helping them manage job costs efficiently. Additionally, users value the integration with accounting software like Xero, which enhances their overall financial management.

Invoicing

32% Positive

58% Neutral

10% Negative

Users find Simpro's invoicing features efficient and versatile, allowing them to manage and update invoices seamlessly. They appreciate the ability to handle recurring invoices with ACH and credit card options, which simplifies their billing processes. Additionally, users value the ease of importing supplier invoices and the flexibility to delete or modify invoices as needed.

## Simpro FAQs

- What is Simpro used for?







Simpro is used to streamline a range of field service operations. These may include estimating jobs, setting up appointments for technicians, keeping track of inventory, and building financial and performance reports. Any organization that dispatches field technicians to different job sites can use Simpro to manage their workforce and jobs.

- Is Simpro an ERP system?







Simpro is not an ERP system. An ERP typically comes with a range of modules for managing a broad range of business functions. Simpro, on the other hand, focuses on creating smoother workflows for field service professionals and managers.

- Does Simpro track location?







Yes, Simpro comes with a location tracking feature. Using this, you can monitor the location of field technicians and the vehicles they operate. This can speed up the process of choosing the right person for each job, because you can pick the one closest to the customer’s location.

- Is Simpro easy to use?







Simpro comes with a lot of features, which may make it difficult to learn quickly. At the same time, Simpro also provides learning resources to speed up the onboarding process. It also features a user-friendly interface that many find fairly easy to learn.

- Does Simpro have a mobile app?







Yes, Simpro has a mobile app that field techs can use to get job information, manage inventory, keep track of time spent on the job, and more. The mobile function also serves as a link between those in the field and headquarters because it gives users real-time updates as they enter data.
