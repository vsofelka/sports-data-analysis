# Key Entities — Companies, Competitors, Personas, Products

## Simpro Group — Core Entities

### simPRO (Product)
The flagship FSM platform. Core modules include:
- **Job Management:** Create, assign, and track jobs from quote to invoice
- **Scheduling & Dispatch:** Drag-and-drop scheduler, GPS tracking, mobile app for field techs
- **Quoting & Estimating:** Pre-built catalog items, labor rates, markup rules
- **Invoicing & Payments:** Automated invoicing, Stripe/payment gateway integration
- **Reporting & Dashboards:** 100+ built-in reports; custom report builder
- **Inventory Management:** Parts, equipment, and warehouse tracking
- **Customer Portal:** Self-service job status and invoice access for end customers

### BuildXact (Product)
Estimating and project management software for residential builders and remodelers. Acquired by Simpro in 2021. Targets a different buyer (builders) than simPRO (trade contractors).

### Clockshark (Product)
Time tracking and crew scheduling tool for field service and construction. Acquired by Simpro in 2021. Lighter-weight than simPRO; targets smaller teams who need time tracking without full FSM.

## Competitors

### ServiceTitan
- **HQ:** Glendale, CA
- **Market:** HVAC, plumbing, electrical, garage door — residential and commercial
- **Size:** ~3,000 employees; went public (TTAN) December 2024
- **Differentiator:** Strongest US brand recognition, deepest residential service integrations, heavy marketing/sales investment
- **Weakness:** More expensive than simPRO, complex implementation, US-only focus
- **vs. Simpro:** ServiceTitan dominates the US residential trade market; Simpro is stronger in commercial/industrial and ANZ

### Jobber
- **HQ:** Edmonton, Canada
- **Market:** Small home service businesses (1–25 employees)
- **Differentiator:** Simple, fast onboarding; strong mobile UX; lower price point (~$50–$300/month)
- **Weakness:** Limited features for larger or more complex contractors; no inventory management
- **vs. Simpro:** Jobber targets smaller businesses than simPRO's sweet spot

### FieldEdge (formerly dESCO)
- **HQ:** Fort Myers, FL
- **Market:** HVAC and plumbing contractors
- **Differentiator:** Long-tenured player, strong accounting integrations
- **Weakness:** Older UI; slower innovation cycle than simPRO or ServiceTitan
- **vs. Simpro:** Competes in similar mid-market space, but simPRO has broader geographic reach

### HouseCallPro
- **HQ:** San Diego, CA
- **Market:** Residential home service (small businesses)
- **Differentiator:** Strong consumer-facing booking and review tools; easy setup
- **vs. Simpro:** Consumer/residential focus vs. simPRO's commercial/B2B trade focus

### Salesforce Field Service (formerly ClickSoftware)
- **Market:** Enterprise field service (utilities, telecoms, large contractors)
- **Differentiator:** Deep CRM integration; enterprise-grade compliance and workflow
- **vs. Simpro:** Higher price point, requires Salesforce CRM; targets larger enterprises than simPRO's typical customer

## Key Buyer Personas

### Operations Manager / Service Manager
- **Role:** Oversees scheduling, dispatch, and job completion
- **Pain points:** Technician no-shows, job status visibility, rescheduling chaos
- **Buys simPRO for:** Scheduling board, GPS tracking, mobile job app

### Business Owner / GM (SMB)
- **Role:** Final decision-maker for software purchases
- **Pain points:** Cash flow gaps from slow invoicing, no visibility into job profitability
- **Buys simPRO for:** Automated invoicing, real-time reporting, quoting accuracy

### Finance / Accounts Manager
- **Role:** Manages invoicing, payments, and reconciliation
- **Pain points:** Manual data entry between FSM and accounting software, late payments
- **Buys simPRO for:** Xero/QuickBooks integration, automated invoice delivery

### Revenue Operations Analyst (Simpro internal buyer of this project's insights)
- **Role:** Delivers pipeline reporting, owns CRM data quality, supports sales leadership
- **Pain points:** Siloed CRM data, no self-service dashboards, manual Board reporting
- **Uses this project for:** Pipeline health monitoring, lead source ROI, deal velocity tracking
